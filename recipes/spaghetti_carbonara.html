Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 200g / 7 oz. of spaghetti
  • 100g / 3.5 oz. of pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g / 1.75 oz. of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, minced garlic, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta. Toss until the sauce thickens and coats the pasta.
  6. Serve immediately with extra grated Parmesan cheese and black pepper.
